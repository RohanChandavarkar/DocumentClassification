  There are different kinds of games and sports.  There are indoor games as cards, chess, ludo ect.  Out of the out-door games,Hockey, Football, Volleyball, Cricket, Tennis, etc. are the more common ones.  Races, Jumps, Throws, Weight-lifting are some of the other kinds of the sports.  Games and sports are necessary and useful for all.  They are specially useful for the students who must have a balanced development of the body as well as the mind.  A sound mind lives only in a sound body. “All work and no play,” as the proverb goes, “ makes Jack a dull boy.” Games and sports contribute to  an all round development of personality and develop such qualities as help in the formation of a noble character.
